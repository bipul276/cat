Some of the most common cat breeds are :-

Domestic Shorthair: These cats are of mixed ancestry and come in various coat colors and patterns. They are known for their friendly and adaptable nature.

Domestic Longhair: Similar to domestic shorthairs but with longer fur. They require regular grooming to keep their coats in good condition.

Fun Facts

Whisker Magic: Cats use their whiskers not only for balance but also to determine if they can fit through a tight space. If their whiskers touch the sides, they know they won't fit.

Superior Night Vision: Cats have exceptional night vision, about six times better than that of humans. This ability is thanks to a layer of cells in their eyes called the tapetum lucidum, which reflects light.
